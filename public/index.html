Real Estate

The Firm’s real estate practice represents investors, developers, joint ventures and private equity funds in commercial and residential real estate transactions, shepherding clients through the acquisition, development, sale, leasing and/or financing of residential and commercial real estate. Our team is well versed in negotiating, drafting and/or reviewing organizational documents, including operating, partnership and joint venture agreements, promissory notes, mortgages, financing statements and guarantees, as well as third party agreements with architects, engineers, contractors, real estate brokers and other professionals.

In addition to the transactional aspect of its real estate practice, the Firm handles litigation matters on behalf of lenders, brokers, purchasers and sellers, including real estate holding companies, joint ventures and developers. The Firm has also represented clients in the prosecution or defense of loan defaults, mortgage foreclosures, fraud-based claims, breach of contract disputes, claims alleging real estate joint ventures or partnerships, claims for breach of contracted services and claims involving improper liens or clouds on title to real property.

Corporate Defense

The Firm’s corporate practice is well versed in providing legal advice regarding commercial transactions, including mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures and the like. The Firm’s attorneys routinely negotiate and prepare purchase, merger, operating and/or partnership agreements as well as debt, equity and asset instruments or agreements relating to the rendering of services or the manufacture, procurement and/or distribution of goods. Additionally, the Firm provides support in the negotiating, drafting and/or reviewing of employment agreements, including independent contractor, consulting, non-compete and non-disparagement agreements.

As part of the Firm’s business consulting practice, it is common for our attorneys to address corporate officers, boards of directors and personnel in a coordinated effort to provide counsel on the fiduciary obligations of corporate officers or in the acquisition, merger, liquidation and/or winding-down of operations to ensure compliance with organizational agreements and applicable law.

Commercial Litigation

The Firm’s commercial litigation practice is adept in handling complex civil and commercial litigation matters, ranging from pre-suit settlements and dispute resolution alternatives to the prosecution or defense of contentious litigation. The Firm’s attorneys have handled a diverse array of litigation proceedings including, but not limited to, class actions, contract disputes, consumer finance claims, product liability and mass tort defects, vicarious liability and indemnity demands, unfair and deceptive trade practices, insurance coverage issues, commercial landlord-tenant disputes, and director and officer liability proceedings.

To date, the Firm’s legal team has successfully represented Fortune 500 companies, publicly traded and privately held corporations, as well as financial institutions, organizations providing oil and natural resources, real estate developers, international importers, and local small-business owners. Recognizing that each client’s needs are unique is paramount to the Firm’s overall holistic approach—a conscious awareness of economic and non-economic objectives together with an overall aversion to unnecessary risk, all while providing cost-effective approaches to everyday problem solving.

Business Consulting

The Firm’s business consulting and crisis management practice represents individuals and corporations ranging from start-up companies to large corporations. Indeed, our team has extensive experience advising corporations and their officers on acquisitions, mergers and joint ventures. The Firm’s lawyers are also well versed in employment matters, including drafting and negotiating employment, non-compete, non-disparagement and confidentiality agreements.

Our background in corporate structure, coupled with almost two decades of combined litigation experience, allows the Firm to provide pre-litigation counseling, risk prevention and real-time solutions to its clients, avoiding costly business interruptions and unnecessary litigation expenses. In moments when litigation is unforeseen or unavoidable, the Firm’s crisis management division stands resolute in its effort to lessen the impact adverse litigation may have on the business, its officers, employees, profits and assets.
